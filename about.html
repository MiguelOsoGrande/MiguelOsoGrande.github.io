About Me

My name is Miguel Angel Sheppard, a Cuban and Dominican graphic designer and illustrator from Roxbury, Boston. I'm passionate about design, growth, and storytelling, and I see my work as a way to spark emotion and build deeper connections. Influenced by comics, art, music, and fashion, I strive to blend these elements to create compelling visuals.

My artistic journey began here in Boston, where I developed my skills through creative programs and collaborations. As a student at Boston Arts Academy, I honed my craft while working with the nonprofit Artists for Humanity.

Since then, I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with various brands and businesses, shaping visual identities and contributing to Boston’s evolving design culture.
