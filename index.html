Hello, and welcome!

My name is Miguel Angel Sheppard. I Hail from the streets of Roxbury, Boston, Massachusetts. My life revolves around my deep-seated passions for Art, Design, and Fashion. From the earliest stages of my journey, these interests have not only been my hobbies but have evolved into the very essence of my being.


I've been blessed with the opportunity to attempt to try and balance both the rigors of university and the demands of the professional world, I take great pride in seamlessly integrating my studies with my work. It's an incredible privilege to pursue my dreams while continuing to expand my knowledge and skills in design.


On this site, you'll have the chance to follow my creative odyssey as I engage in a multitude of multi-disciplinary activities in the realm of design in order to create compelling and innovative projects.
